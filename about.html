About Movie Hub Pro

Movie Hub Pro is a volunteer-run curation platform focused on films that are freely available for viewing and study. Our curators locate, verify, and describe public-domain and Creative Commons films from trustworthy archives, adding contextual notes and suggested uses for educators and researchers.

Why we exist

Many valuable historical and educational films are scattered across archives and repositories. Our goal is to make them discoverable, explain their provenance, and provide guidance on reuse and citation.

Who runs this site

Founders: Gabriel Sakala & Kingsley Mudenda. If you'd like to suggest a collection or report a problem, please use the contact page.
